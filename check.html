Elite Auto Shipping is a Staten Island, New York-based privately held corporation. Since 2008, the company has been in operation. Elite Auto Shipping is a fully certified, bonded, and insured vehicle transport company. Its USDOT number is 2242996, and its MC number is 656874. The company carries over-sized cars, trailers, SUVs, and ATVs throughout 48 states. They also transport heavy equipment and boats. Elite Auto Shipping provides both door-to-door and terminal-to-terminal services. To transport the automobiles to their destination, they utilise one truck and one driver. Students relocating to college and military families receive discounted services from the company. For expedited services, cars are delivered in 1 to 3 days.
